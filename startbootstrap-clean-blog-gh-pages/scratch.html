Reasons I love traveling

  1. Spend quality time with people I love
  2. Immerse myself in cultures all over the world
  3. Try out all of the different food in other areas of the world

I feel immensely grateful for all of the experiences I have had throughout my life traveling around the world. Some of my best memories I have with my family have taken place while on vacation. Seeing the way other people live, especailly in thrid world countries helps me to be grateful for what I have been given and what I have been blessed with. It is a ton of fun to visit historical places in France and England and see all of the castles and archtiecture. The Bahamas and New Zealand had beautiful beaches and lots of extreme sports to participate in such as bungee jumping and white water rafting. In Brazil and Ecuador I was able to see lots of different animals and try lots of different types of food.
